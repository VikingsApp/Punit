SUSTAINING A HEALTHIER ANIMAL WORLD



Brilliant Bio Pharma Private limited (Brilliant), the fastest growing animal health company in India, started its journey just before the turn of the millennium, in 1998. Traversing the challenging path of biotech to develop products and provide services of international standards to the global animal health industry, the company has emerged as one of the world’s largest modernized manufacturers of the FMD vaccine, veterinary anti-rabies vaccines and other specific vaccines for the bovine, canine and ovine species. Brilliant is a major stakeholder in Govt. of India's FMD Control Programme.

Brilliant is the first company in India to produce FMD vaccines in 10KL fermenters in BSL-3 facility. While its 10KL capacity Bioreactors for cell propagation and virus production are a first in India, Brilliant’s Biosafety Level 3 BSL3 containment manufacturing facility is one of the largest facilities in the world. From 2.30 lakh in 2005 to over 1.5 billion today, the company’s vaccines have immunized and protected animals across all species around the globe. It also has earned distinction as a Star Export House.
