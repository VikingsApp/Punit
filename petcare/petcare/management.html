From the Chairman’s Desk




At Brilliant, we are committed to the development of clinical innovations rooted in veterinary medical needs, evidence-based research, and an understanding of future requirements. Our journey from 230,000 doses at inception to 1.5 billion doses today has been one of discovery, dedication and dream-building.

We will continue to pursue our mission of improving the availability and affordability of efficacious veterinary vaccines and health products to protect our animal friends from known and emerging infectious diseases and keep them healthy. My team and I endeavour to be a one stop solution for all things animal health!






Beacons of Progress





Mourya Boda, Chairman Mourya has a Graduate Degree in Law and Masters in Business Management that have contributed immensely to her success as an animal biotech entrepreneur. Presently a Governing Body Member of FICCI FLO, New Delhi and Blue Cross of Hyderabad, she has chaired Young FICCI Ladies Organization, Hyderabad Chapter in the past

Over the last decade and a half of her entrepreneurial journey, Mourya has comprehended the immense value that a company can generate by putting its people ahead of profits through shared values such as dependability, intrapreneurship, ownership, responsible freedom and collaboration. That she has been able to leverage this emotional currency as a sustainable model is evident from the cohesiveness that the various teams display in their day-to-day working style

Mourya attributes happy customers to happy employees. Since the early stages, Mourya has championed the transformation of Brilliant’s culture into one focused on bringing out the best in its people. The culture today at Brilliant – of an equal playing ground, the environment of innovation, a highly approachable management style, employee recognition and dignity, and the deep respect for a value-based way of life – has enabled both the organisation and its employees to thrive.
